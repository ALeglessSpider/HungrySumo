How To Play?

You have octadirectional movement. To gain points, eat fresh food and avoid rotten food. If you come in contact with a pufferfish you lose a life. You lose weight and point if you eat rotten food.

Controls

Arrow up: Go up
Arrow down: Go down
Arrow left: Go left
Arrow right: Go right
Space: Quit
